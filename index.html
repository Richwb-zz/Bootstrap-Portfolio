About Me

The Coder With-in

After developing an interest in computers at an early age I have made in my life's goal to grow with technology and follow the trends, that is what has led me to this junction in life. After years of self-taught HTML, CSS, PhP and other web development languages I realized I was missing the vital link to turn websites from static pages with databases to breathing entities, javascript. I decided to take the plunge into a language i always shyed away from to become a full stack developer. I also knew that self teaching can only go so far and sometimes you fight yourself so I decided to enroll in a Javascript bootcamp to give me that extra push I needed.

You will find in this site my portfolio encompassing all that I have learned during my time at the bootcamp, along with materials that I have learned on my own along the way. My skills and abilities have grown stronger and stronger to develop a wide range of javascript and node.js based entities readying myself for my future career and further expand my horizons in this limitless field.

The Person Behind the Code

It's hard to grow up in north-western NJ and not loving to be outdoors, after all you can't get anywhere without a car or bicycle. While I can fairly say I certainly spent a good deal of time playing video games and tinkering on my computer it is nice to get away from it all every once and a while and explorer ones on backwoods til dusk falls. More recently I have taken a joy in Geocaching and Scuba diving. Nothing beats the thrill of being underwater in near complete silence. I've always had a love of the water swimming, Kayaking, scuba diving. I should move to Florida...
